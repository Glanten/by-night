Nosferatu

The Nosferatu wear their curse on the outside, their bodies horribly twisted and deformed; however, they are adept at using their supernatural abilities to see everything whilst going unseen

Disciplines

Animalism
Obfuscate
Potence

Bane: Repulsiveness

Nosferatu characters must taken the Repulsive flaw and may never raise the rating of their Looks merit

A Nosferatu character suffers a dice pool penalty equal to their Bane Severity when attempting to disguise themselves (including the use of Disciplines such as Mask of a Thousand Faces)

Most Nosferatu are perceived as hideous or terrifying when witnessed by mortals
