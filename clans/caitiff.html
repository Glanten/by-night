Caitiff

Sometimes a vampire does not inherit the traits of their sire's clan; these individuals are marked as Caitiff and are often abandoned by their sire and treated like dross

Disciplines

Caitiff characters have access to all Disciplines at character creation, but they buy Disciplines for a flat cost when spending Experience.

Bane: Outcast

The Caitiff character begins with the Suspect flaw and they may not purchase positive status during Character Creation

The Storyteller may impose a -1 or -2 dice pool penalty to social tests among vampires who know the character is Caitiff

For Caitiff characters, improving a Discipline costs Experience equal to 6× the Discipline's level
