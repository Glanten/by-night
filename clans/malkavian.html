Malkavian

Malkavians often have the gift of deep insight, but are also are touched by madness that comes from seeing too much of the world at once, understanding too deeply, and feeling emotions too strong to bear

Disciplines

Auspex
Dominate
Obfuscate

Bane: Fractured Perspective

If a Malkavian character suffers a Bestial Failure or Compulsion, their mental derangement inflicts a penalty to one category of dice pools (Physical, Social, or Mental; chosen at character creation) equal to their Bane Severity for one scene

Compulsion: Delusion

For one scene, the Malkavian suffers -2 to dice pools involving Dexterity, Manipulation, Composure, Wits, and rolls to resist Terror Frenzy
