Gangrel

A clan of survivalists able to thrive in any environment, the Gangrel are more concerned with action and accomplishments than appearance and status

Disciplines

Animalism
Fortitude
Protean

Bane: Bestial Features

A Gangrel character gains a number of Bestial Features equal to their Bane Severity whenever they Frenzy; each Bestial Feature reduces an Attribute by 1

Bestial Features last the night of the Frenzy and the subsequent night

A Gangrel character who chooses to Ride the Wave during a Frenzy develops a maximum of 1 Bestial Feature
