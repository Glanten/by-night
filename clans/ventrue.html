Ventrue

The Ventrue are considered the aristocrats of the vampire world, willing to endure storms for the sake of leading from the front

Disciplines

Dominate
Fortitude
Presence

Bane: Rarified Tastes

The Ventrue character has a specific type of mortal from whom they prefer to feed (natural redheads, people born in acertain city, vegans, celebrities, etc.)

A Resolve + Awareness test can sense if a mortal fits the character's feeding preference

Feeding from outside the character's preference will result in vomiting up the blood unless the Ventrue character spends Willpower equal to their Bane Severity
