Thin-Bloods

Neither fully vampire not wholly mortal; considered by some to be heralds of the final nights, the Thin-Bloods are pitied, envied, and feared in equal measures

Disciplines

Thin-Blood Alchemy

Thin-Blood Special Rules

Thin-Blood characters must take between 1 and 3 Thin-Blood Merits, and the same number of Thin-Blood Flaws at character creation.

Thin-Blood characters have Blood Potency 0 which means that they:

  • Take damage as a mortal
  • Cannot create Blood Bonds
  • Cannot create Ghouls
  • Cannot predictably turn a mortal into a vampire
  • Only Frenzy in reaction to supernatural effects
  • Only take 1 point of Superficial Damage in direct sunlight
  • Always count as having used the Blush of Life
