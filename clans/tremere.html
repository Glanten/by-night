Tremere

Scheming sorcerers, Clan Tremere jealously guards and willfuly employs the secrets of Blood Sorcery to further its enigmatic agendas

Disciplines

Auspex
Blood Sorcery
Dominate

Bane: Deficient Blood

Tremere characters are unable to Blood Bond other vampires (although they can still be bound by those of other clans)

A Tremere character can Blood Bond a mortal, but must give additional Vitae equal to their Bane Severity

Compulsion: Perfectionism

Until the end of the scene, or until rolling a critical win on a skill roll, the Tremere character suffers a -2 penalty to all dice pools

This penalty is reduced to -1 for actions that are a repetition of previous ones, and removed entirely on a second repetition
