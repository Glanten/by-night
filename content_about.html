By Night is an introduction to V5 of Vampire: The Masquerade to ease character creation for new V5 players.

Rules, lore, and other details were interpreted from the V5 core rulebook and the Players Guide. Many elements were omitted for brevity or occluded to avoid spoilers for those wishing to discover the game through play.

Vampire: The Masquerade and its setting, The World of Darkness, are owned by Paradox Interactive who maintain a solid V5 wiki.
