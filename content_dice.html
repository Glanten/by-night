Dice

All dice rolled should be ten-sided dice (d10)

Success and Failure

A result of 1 to 5 on a dice is a failure

A result of 6 to 0 on a dice is a success

Dice Pools

Attributes, Skills, Disciplines, and other Traits are measured in dots • each of which represents one dice in a character's dice pool

When asked to roll a pool of dice (e.g. "roll Willpower"), you roll as many dice as you have • in a Trait and count the number of successes

Other checks are rolled as dice pooled from multiple Traits (e.g. "roll Intelligence + Technology") which means you roll as many dice as you have in both Traits combined

Criticals

When rolling a pool, every pair of 0s you roll is a Critical Success and counts as an additional 2 successes.
