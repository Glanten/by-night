Incurring Stains on a character's Humanity, but doing so in the service of one of their Convictions, reduces the Stains by 1 or more

Violating a Conviction may, at the Storyteller's discretion, incur 1 or more Stains depending on the violation's severity

Example Convictions

Thou shalt not kill Kill only in self-defence Never expose children to violence
Sabotage the powerful Courage is the highest virtue Keep your word
Slavery is evil My country, right or wrong Never do drugs
Do not commit torture The guilty must be punished Always claim what you are due
Reject power, for it corrupts Always aid women in need Take the law into your own hands
Keep the old traditions alive My survival comes first Never snitch

Touchstones

A Touchstone is a mortal NPC who embodies a Conviction; they may be someone your character knew in life, or someone they have come across since becoming a vampire

Significantly benefiting one of your Touchstones, despite it being against your best interests, may remove Aggravated Willpower damage from your character

Damaging or destroying a Touchstone incurs Stains on your character's Humanity
