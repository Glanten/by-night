Some Skills have a Specialty. If you attempt a task that falls within a relevant Skill's Speciality, you gain an extra dice for the roll.

If you put dots in Academics, Craft, Performance, and Science you get a free Speciality in that Skill.

To assign your character's Skills, pick one of the methods below:
