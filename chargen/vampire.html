So that was your life, and then some monster killed you...

Blood Potency

How strong is the unnatural power coursing through your veins? Your Blood Potency depends on your generation:
  • 10th or 11th Generation: Blood Potency 2
  • 12th or 13th Generation: Blood Potency 1
  • 14th or higher Generation: Blood Potency 0
Characters typically begin with Blood Potency 1. Only Thin-Blood characters have Blood Potency 0.

Humanity

How close are you to what you once were? How strong is the Beast's hold over you?
Unless directed otherwise by your Storyteller, your initial Humanity should be 7.
