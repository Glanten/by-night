Think about your morals and ethics. What do you believe in? Who is important to you?

Convictions

A Conviction is a guiding light for you to follow, a line you will not cross; a good Conviction typically starts with "Always..." or "Never..." and should be likely to come up in play

Create between 1 and 3 Convictions for your character

Did you adopt these Convictions from your family? Did you adopt them from a mentor or partner? Perhaps you developed them during one of your significant life events?

Example Convictions

  • "Always keep your sworn word"
  • "Never trust someone who earns more than you"
  • "Thou shalt not kill"
  • "Slavery is evil"
  • "I only care about me and mine"
  • "No meat without feet"

Touchstones

A Touchstone is a mortal who shines as an example of one of your Convictions

Create one Touchstone for each of your Convictions

Record your Touchstones on the Relationship Map

Ambition

What was your life's greatest aspiration? This should be something grand, measurable, and achievable

Note that your life's Ambition may change dramatically after your character becomes a vampire

Desire

Your character's Desire represents a short-term goal; a good Desire is linked to someone on the Relationship Map

You may define your Desire at the beginning of every session; it is perfectly acceptable to pursue an unfulfilled Desire from a past session
