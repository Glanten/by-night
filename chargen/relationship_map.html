The Relationship Map is a social diagram connecting important characters in your Chronicle.

You should work with the Storyteller and your fellow players to draw the Relationship Map during character creation.

Establish Coterie Relationships

Write the names of each member of the Coterie (i.e. the Player Characters) on the map.

Give each member of the Coterie the following:

  • One positive relationship with someone else from the Coterie
  • One negative relationship with someone else from the Coterie

A positive relationship might be something like "fellow cat lover"; a negative relationship might be something like "doesn't trust rich kids"

Sires

You may come back to this step after picking your character's Clan.

Each member of the Coterie writes the name of their sire on the Relationship Map and draws an arrow from their sire to their character.

You may wish to note your sire's Clan, how long (roughly) they've been a vampire, known Sect position, and the nature of your character's fateful encounter with them.

Supporting Sect Characters

If your characters are newly-turned Fledglings, you may wish to skip this step.

Each member of the Coterie should have connections with two more vampires; they may be someone else's sire, an existing support character, or one you create with the Storyteller.
