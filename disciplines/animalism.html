Animalism

This discipline confers power over animals, particularly creatures of the night. Those who master this power might surround themselves with swarms of bats, transfer their consciousness into the body of a cat or coyote, draw additional sustenance from the blood of animals, or manipulate the Beast within other vampires.

Type: Mental

Level 1

Bond Famulus

Cost: Free once the Blood Bond is established

This power allows a vampire to create a particularly strong Blood Bond with a single animal. The resulting famulus is fantastically loyal, and several other Animalism powers can be used on it without cost.

Sense the Beast

Cost: Free

The vampire can detect the Beast within others, their hostility, and sometimes even their specific nature or the resonance of their blood.

Level 2

Feral Whispers

Cost: 1 Rouse Check per animal type (free when used on a famulus)

The vampire can engage in two-way communication with animals and may summon animals to attend them from nearby.
