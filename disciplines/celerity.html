Celerity

Vampires using Celerity can move with blinding speed, dodge bullets, run up walls or across water, and strike in melee faster than their opponents can react.

Type: Physical

Level 1

Cat's Grace

Cost: Free

So long as a surface can take your weight, you automatically pass tests to keep your balance.

Rapid Reflexes

Cost: Free

You suffer no penalty to dodge firearm attacks when you are without cover. You may also combine minor actions with other actions during your turn without penalty.

Level 2

Fleetness

Cost: 1 Rouse Check

The vampire adds their Celerity to non-combat Dexterity tests and, once per turn, when using their supernatural agility to defend themselves in combat.
