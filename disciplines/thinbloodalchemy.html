Thin-Blood Alchemy

While not a Discipline in the strictest sense, Thin-Bloods have developed a unique method for both emulating more powerful vampiric abilities and developing unique powers of their own. Thin-Blood Alchemy is composed of formulae that must be prepared in advance, then consumed to take effect. The alchemist's Blood is always required, and most formulae also require mortal blood of a specific resonance.

Level 1

Far Reach

Activation Cost: 1 Rouse Check

The alchemist becomes capable of limited telekinesis, able to lift and manupilate objects (and, with effort, even people) at a distance. Causing direct harm with this power is difficult, but creative alchemists may boast about pulling safety pins, pumping brakes, and toppling heavy bookcases to deal with their enemies.

Haze

Activation Cost: 1 Rouse Check

This formula creates a mist that shrouds the alchemist, making them difficult to make out. The mist follows the user wherever they go and can be extended to cloud others nearby at an additional cost.

Level 2

Counterfiet (Level 1)

At this level, an alchemist can develop formulae to reproduce the powers of level 1 Discipline. Each formula takes a week to research.

Envelop

Activation Cost: 1 Rouse Check

The alchemist can release a cloud of fog that clings to a target, blinding and choking them. This power only affects a single target, and the alchemist may only affect one victim at a time.
