Obfuscate

Some vampires can melt into the shadows, walk through a crowd unnoticed, or change their appearance. This is a form of illusion, and will not persist if the user draws attention to themselves, bumps into someone, or makes a great deal of noise. It also does not work on electronic surveillance such as cameras and microphones, but masters of this Discipline have been known to bypass even these limitations.

Type: Mental

Level 1

Cloak of Shadows

Cost: Free

So long as they stand perfectly still, the vampire remains completely invisible to the naked eye.

Silence of Death

Cost: Free

This power removes all sound from the vampire, silencing their footsteps, clothing, and so forth. A dropped object or slammed door would still make a noise, but sprinting footfalls or the racking slide of a shotgun would go entirely unnoticed.

Level 2

Unseen Passage

Cost: 1 Rouse Check

So long as they emit no overpowering odour and make no sound louder than a whisper, the vampire can move about without being seen.
