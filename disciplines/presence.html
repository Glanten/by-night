Presence

Presence targets emotions, not thoughts, so victims remain cognisant (unlike the victims of Dominate) but their behaviour is not always predictable. Dice modifiers from Presence powers do not stack. Masters of Presence can convey their enchanting auras through broadcast media such as radio or television, compel distant mortals to rush to their side, or instil terror with a single glance.

Type: Mental

Level 1

Awe

Cost: Free

The vampire adds their Presence to Charisma-related rolls (Persuasion, Performance, etc.), stealing the limelight to subtly affect the reactions and opinions of those around them.

Daunt

Cost: Free

Almost the opposite of Awe, while this Discipline is active the vampire adds their Presence to Intimidation rolls and those who would attack the vampire must first overcome a subconscious sense of dread.

Level 2

Lingering Kiss

Cost: Free

When a vampire drinks someone's blood and employs this power, their victim obsesses over the sensation, suffering withdrawal if they cannot find the vampire on subsequent nights. This can lead to easy feeds, but can be insidiously dangerous if used on another vampire.
