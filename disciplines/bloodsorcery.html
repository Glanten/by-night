Blood Sorcery

Employed almost exclusively by Clan Tremere, Blood Sorcery (or "Thaumaturgy" as they call it) is unlike other Disciplines in that it must be learned (normally from a mentor, but increasingly from books and other records) rather than evolved or adapted. Blood Sorcery employs genuine magic that no mortal could ever hope to wield. Learning this Discipline allows grants access to Rituals that, while lengthy and complicated, often prove extremely versatile.

Type: Sorcery

Level 1

Corrosive Vitae

Cost: 1 Rouse Check

Each Rouse Check allows the vampire to spill blood that corrodes 35cm3 of material over 5 minutes. This does no damage to living (or supernatural) beings, and harder materials such as copper or cast iron might take longer to dissolve (alloys and steel often simply pit and scar).

A Taste for Blood

Cost: Free

By tasting a drop of blood, the vampire can determine a number of vital information about its owner such as their nature (e.g. mortal, vampire, ghoul, or other supernatural creature), Blood Potency, vampiric generation, and so forth.

Level 2

Extinguish Vitae

Cost: Free

With concentration and a gesture, the user can increase another vampire's Hunger by curdling the Blood within their body, effectively starving them from a distance.

Scour Secrets

Cost: 1 Rouse Check

Although it leaves a telltale bloodstain behind, the sorcerer can use their blood to search through written material to reveal information far quicker than they could by reading individual items.
