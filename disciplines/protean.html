Protean

One of the most overt Disciplines, Protean allows a vampire to change their physical form. Masters of Protean can adopt the form of a wolf or a bat, safely entomb themselves in the earth, or turn their body into a cloud of ethereal mist. Clothes and suchlike are transformed with the vampire, but carried items are not, so most Protean users prefer to travel light.

Type: Physical

Level 1

Eyes of the Beast

Cost: Free

With an intimidating red gleam to their eyes, the vampire ignores all sight penalties imposed by darkness (natural or otherwise).

Weight of the Feather

Cost: Free

The vampire can reduce their effective mass and density so as to become almost weightless. This can be invaluable when plummetting great distances, avoiding pressure plates, or suffering a throw or collision.

Level 2

Feral Weapons

Cost: 1 Rouse Check

With long claws or dagger-like fangs, the vampire inflicts more damage with unarmed attacks and Superficial Damage they inflict with unarmed attacks is not halved.
