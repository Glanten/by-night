Fortitude

A vampire's undead body is more resilient than it was in life, but vampires that master Fortitude can shrug off shotgun blasts, walk through fires, and shield their minds from powers that would probe their thoughts.

Type: Physical

Level 1

Resilience

Cost: Free

The vampire adds their Fortitude to their Health, making them even harder to kill.

Unswayable Mind

Cost: Free

The vampire adds their Fortitude to their attempts to resist coercion, intimidation, subterfuge, and supernatural powers (e.g. Dominate or Presence) that would otherwise influence their mind.

Level 2

Toughness

Cost: 1 Rouse Check

When this power is active, the vampire reduces all incoming Superficial Damage by their Fortitude, although even this power has limits.

Enduring Beasts

Amalgam: Requires Animalism 1

Cost: Free (for a famulus), or 1 Rouse Check

Animals affected by the vampire's Animalism powers gain Health equal to the vampire's Fortitude
