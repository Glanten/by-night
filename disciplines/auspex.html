Auspex

Auspex is concerned with discerning truth from lies, probing the minds of those around the user, and perceiving the invisible. Masters of this Discipline are aware of everything around them, can communicate without speaking, and can completely possess mortal bodies.

Type: Mental

Level 1

Heightened Senses

Cost: Free

See in the dark, even in pitch blackness, hear untrasonic frequencies or distance conversations, and smell the fear of cowering prey.

Sense the Unseen

Cost: Free

Perceive supernatural presences otherwise hidden from the naked eye.

Level 2

Premonition

Cost: 1 Rouse Check if used proactively, free when used passively

The vampire may experience flashes of insightm guiding them to something hidden or secret.
