Potence

Vampires with Potence find their bodies capable of incredible feats of strength. Masters of Potence are able to scale sheer stone walls, shake the earth with their might, or incite rage in those around them.

Type: Physical

Level 1

Lethal Body

Cost: Free

The vampire's unarmed attacks infilct Aggravated Damage to mortals and ignore armour based on the user's Potence.

Soaring Leap

Cost: Free

The vampire can make leaps no mortal could dream of accomplishing. The maximum height and length, in meters, of such jumps are 3 × Potence and 5 × Potence respectively.

Level 2

Prowess

Cost: 1 Rouse Check

While this power is active, the vampire adds their Potence to feats of strength, their unarmed attack damage, and half their Potence to the damage they inflict with melee weapons.
