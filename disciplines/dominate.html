Dominate

Making eye contact with some vampires is all it takes to fall completely under their thrall. The victim of a Dominate power becomes a mindless puppet for the duration of their domination, and so there are limits to what Dominate can achieve (i.e. victims cannot follow commands that rely on their memory or judgement). Nevertheless, masters of this power can alter memories, direct crowds, and overcome their victims' instincts of self-preservation.

Type: Mental

Level 1

Cloud Memory

Cost: Free

All the but strongest willed victims of this power forget the last few minutes ever happened.

Compel

Cost: Free

This power allows a vampire to issue a short, simple command (e.g.: "kneel", "turn around", "give me that"), typically something that can be accomplished in one turn, which the unprepared mortal mind has almost no chance to resist.

Slavish Devotion

Amalgam: Requires Fortitude 1

Cost: Free

Your Dominated victims are harder for others to Dominate.

Level 2

Mesmerise

Cost: 1 Rouse Check

Similar to Compel, but encompassing more complex commands that take longer to complete (e.g.: "follow that car", "protect me", "disassemble your rifle"); such commands are carried out immediately to the best of the victim's ability.

Dementation

Amalgam: Requires Obfuscate 2

Cost: 1 Rouse Check per scene

Disguised as normal conversation, the vampire attacks the Willpower of their victim, which can lead to a nervous breakdown pr psychotic break.

Domitor's Favour

Cost: 1 Rouse Check
